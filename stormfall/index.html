Stormfall

Scrapyard Forest

A co-op survival prototype after a massive tornado. Gather scrap, craft weapons, hold camp, revive allies, destroy the Robot Cat, and push into the Burning Plains.

Player 1: WASD move, E gather/build/revive, F attack, C craft
Player 2: Arrow keys move, / gather/build/revive, . attack, , craft
Objective: gather wood/scrap/med herbs, build camp, craft storm spears, clear spirits, fight Robot Cat, then return to camp to travel onward.
